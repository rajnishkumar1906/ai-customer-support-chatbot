Refund and Cancellation Policies

Refund Policy:
- Refunds are only applicable for annual subscription plans
- Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of initial purchase
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled at any time
- Pro-rated refunds are available for unused months in annual plans
- Refund processing time is 5-10 business days from approval
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method used
- Processing fees, if any, may be deducted from refund amount

Cancellation Policy:
- Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from account settings
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
- Full access to all features is retained until the billing period ends
- No cancellation fees or penalties are charged
- Account data is retained for 90 days after cancellation for reactivation purposes
- After 90 days, data may be permanently deleted per data retention policy
- Reactivation within 90 days restores all previous data and settings

Account Security:
- Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their account credentials
- Use strong, unique passwords for account access
- Enable two-factor authentication for enhanced security
- Report suspicious activity or unauthorized access immediately
- Platform is not responsible for unauthorized access due to compromised user credentials
- Regular security updates and patches are applied automatically

Data Privacy:
- All candidate and user data is encrypted and stored securely
- Platform complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection regulations
- Data retention policy: 1 year for active accounts, 90 days after account cancellation
- Data deletion requests can be submitted to privacy support email
- Candidate and user data is never sold to third parties
- Data sharing only occurs with explicit user consent or as required by law

Terms of Service:

- Violations of terms may result in account suspension or termination
- Fair use policy applies to API usage and interview limits
